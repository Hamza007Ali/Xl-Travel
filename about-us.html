Travel , Explore , Enjoy

Let’s We Create Memories Together

In 2008 we made our first trip and traveling became our love at first sight. Love and excitement was so strong, that we got the idea to provide awesome and affordable travels for everyone. So that is how XL TRAVEL platform was born.

Passion for delivering best experiences for both sides, travelers and hosts. That’s why we created this platform. To help millions of people realize their dreams and make unforgettable memories.

More about our journey

At XL-Travel we think that the meaning of life is to travel, gaining new experience, making new friends, trying new food or preparing in a different way, learning about new cultures, music, religion and so on.

The task of XL-Travel is to enable as many people as possible to travel at the best prices, write and share unforgettable experiences and create memories.

We live and work by this principle

“The world is a book And those who do not travel read only one page”

Team

Travel is all about being creative and so are our team members that are coming from several countries. Every single one of us has high goals and constant improvements in mind, the passion to always deliver only the best and thus we provide a fantastic user experience and an unforgettable and carefree holiday.

All members of the team are united by a great passion for traveling and this is the reason why we thought about everything when designing and creating the platform. Business travel, family travel, entertainment travel, etc.

Over the years, we have traveled a lot in search of practical solutions around the world and tried all kinds of accommodation, which helped us get a crystal clear picture of what the guest needs to make the trip memorable and coming back again and again.

The team invests a lot of its time in education and research, which we believe is the reason for stable and constant growth in the future.

No Commissions At All

XL-Travel allows hosts to add their real estate and rent accommodation on the platform and rent it out completely without extra commissions. This means that neither the hosts nor the guests are charged with additional costs

Save Up To 40%

The guest communicates directly with the hosts and without intermediate links, travel agencies or other intermediaries. In this way, we enable the owner to offer the best price for the accommodation on the market, thus saving the guest up to 40%.

Everything At One Place

We have everything for you in one place. With us you can book everything from small holiday homes to a room in a large hotel chain. We also make sure that the offer satisfies all our guests, whether they are looking for holiday and entertainment destinations or comfortable accommodation for business trips. We have thought of everything!

Virtual travel assistant

You are never alone with us. Do you have a question? No problem, we are here for you! Our virtual travel agents work 24/7 and are ready for all your questions and will help you choose accommodation that will fulfil all your wishes and requirements in the shortest possible time.

All our virtual agents go through several tests, thus ensuring that we work only with the best in the industry.

More then 150.000 visitors per month

We are taking care that every visitor experiences only the best of our platform. Therefore people are returning to us all the time and in most cases XL-Travel is becoming their first choice. Like that we have gained 150.000 visitors per month and growing.

Safe and Easy

XL-Travel platform is easy to use for everyone, no matter if you are a beginner or already a master of online reservations. We are aware that safety of data is really important, therefore we are taking special care of your data and we are constantly reviewing our safety procedures.
